SENIOR MANAGEMENT

Shamim Ahmed Bhatti

Senior General Manager Operations

Shamim Ahmed Bhatti is the Senior General Manager Operations heading Production, Drilling, Reservoir Engineering and Process Departments including Fields Development Projects in close coordination with JV partners and Governmental Agencies.

Mr. Bhatti has worked for more than 48 years in Oil and Gas Industry. After securing his degree in Petroleum & Gas Engineering in year 1972 from University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, he started his career as Assistant Engineer in Sui Gas Transmission Company working at Sui Gas Field, Baluchistan for four years thereafter, continued to work for over 27 years in Oil and Gas Development Company Limited (OGDCL) and got early retirement as Executive Director (Production). He then joined Mari Gas Company Ltd (MGCL) as Senior General Manager for more than 6 years. During his tenure of OGDCL and MGCL, he attended a number of short courses and was involved in number of studies abroad.

Brig. (R) M Saleem

General Manager Coordination

Brig ® Mohammad Saleem holds a BSc (Honors) degree and is a graduate of Command & Staff Colleague, Quetta. He has served in Pakistan Army as a commissioned officer for 28 years and held various command, staff and instructional appointments. He has also served as General Manager Personnel (Human Resources) in Fauji Foundation for five years. He has been associated with the Group since October 2000.

Muhammad Maroof

General Manager Geophysics

Muhammad Maroof holds a B.Sc degree and a six months training in Seismic Interpretation from Canada. In addition, he did several short courses abroad. He has hands on experience of about 54 years in the field of oil and gas.

Mr. Maroof joined PEL in May 2009. He had been working for Oil and Gas Development Company Limited (OGDCL) for about 31 years and retired as Manager Exploration in November 1997. He was responsible for all kinds of exploration activities in one of the three exploration districts of Pakistan including important fields like Qadirpur, Uch, Dodhak, Pirkoh, Loti, Nandpur, Panjpir etc in OGDCL.

Mr. Maroof also served as Exploration Consultant in Petronas Carigali (Pakistan) Ltd. from November 1997 to May 1998. He evaluated several blocks for farm-in opportunities. He then served Mari Petroleum Company Limited (MPCL) as Manager Exploration / General Manager Exploration / Exploration Advisor from May 1998 to April 2009 and was responsible for entire exploration activities in MPCL.

As General Manger Geophysics, Mr. Maroof is responsible for entire geophysical activities in all D&P Leases and exploration blocks of PEL. He is associated with PEL for the last 11 years.

Azam Malik

General Manager Exploration

M.Azam Malik acquired a Master’s Degree in Geology from University of Punjab in 1962 and joined OGDCL as Geologist. He attended various technical courses in USA and France. He is an accomplished Petroleum Geoscientist with over 58 years of practical industry experience having proven track record of oil and gas discoveries.

On research side, Mr. Malik had made valuable contributions in basin study projects and played a leading role in high grade research on Analog Structural Modeling for fold and thrust belts of Pakistan in collaboration with French Petroleum Institute.

Mr.Malik had held various executive positions particularly as Executive Director Exploration OGDCL. He had been OGDCL’s representative for SUPARCO’s Earth Observation Program and for International Gas Union. He had participated in various international symposia and conferences. He has extensive experience of working in North Africa. For about 10 years he had been associated with Algeria’s national oil company, SONATRACH.

After retirement from OGDCL in 2000, Mr. Malik joined Czech Petroleum Company (MND)as its country General Manger in Pakistan and remained associated with till early 2007. He then, joined PEL as GM Exploration and was assigned a number of exploration portfolios in Pakistan as well as three exploration blocks designated as Haha Permits in Morocco. He managed first ever 3D seismic project in Haha exploration acreage, followed by drilling of two exploratory wells.

In 2015, Olympus Petroleum, an affiliate of PEL in Morocco, acquired Abda Doukkala reconnaissance license in central Morocco. Mr. Malik led team of geoscientists for in depth evaluation of its petroleum potential.

M. A. Shahid

Advisor

M. A. Shahid is Advisor to the Director Operations. He holds Master’s Degree (Distinction) in Petroleum Engineering from Heriot Watt University, Edinburgh, UK and a second Master’s Degree (Honors) in Structural Engineering from Asian Institute of Technology, Bangkok, Thailand besides qualifying BS in Civil Engineering (Honors) from Middle East Technical University, Ankara, Turkey.

He has been trained in various disciplines of Petroleum Engineering in USA, UK, France and Indonesia. His work experience includes working for multinational E&P and Service providers like Occidental and Schlumberger respectively.

Mr. Shahid has a rich experience of over 30 years primarily in Oil and Gas upstream activities related to Petroleum & Reservoir Engineering, Well Testing & Completions, Production Optimization and Drilling. He has a very strong petroleum and reservoir engineering background and remained actively involved, throughout his career, in reservoir engineering, reserves estimation, reservoir simulation, economic evaluation and field development of various oil and gas assets.

In 2009, he served British Petroleum to integrate newly acquired assets of MKK from OPI to BP assets. In mid-2010, he joined Ocean Pakistan Limited (OPL) as General Manager (Process and Facilities Engineering). During this period he actively participated in acquiring Zamzama field (BHP) to OPL's assets. Served Petrolink Engineering Services as Director and constructed Sahiwal Oil terminal for Attock Petroleum Limited.

Syed Zahid Anwar

Advisor

Syed Zahid Anwar is Advisor (Process & Facilities) to Director Operations. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering from NED university of Engineering and Technology.

Mr. Anwar has over 40 years of experience in Oil & Gas, Oil Refinery and Petrochemical plants. He has served for over 7 years in Pakistan Refinery Ltd. (Shell) before joining Occidental Petroleum (OXY) in 1987.

In 2002, Mr. Anwar served in Orient Petroleum as General Manager (Production & Facilities) till 2008. During this period large Oil & Gas facilities were developed in Mirpurkhas & Khipro (MKK) concessions, comprising of 160 MMscfd gas processing facilities, LPG recovery plant and a network of pipelines.

His work experience includes not only the development and evaluation of new fields both in sandstone and fractured limestone reservoirs in deep high pressure regions, but also in rejuvenating old and depleted fields through secondary and tertiary recovery methods.

Naveed Akram

General Manager Reservoir Management

Naveed Akram has more than 43 years of experience in domestic and international oil and gas industry. He holds a Bachelor (B.E) in Chemical Engineering and Masters in Petroleum Engineering. He had been working for Aramco, Petroleum Recovery Research Center (PRRC USA) and Pakistan Oilfields Limited (POL).

Mr.Akram has diversified experience in reservoir management, field development, reservoir engineering, reservoir simulation, reservoir economics, E.O.R techniques, production engineering and operations. He is currently serving in PEL as General Manager Reservoir Management.

M. Jamil

General Manager Drilling

Muhammad Jamil completed his Bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering from University of Engineering & Technology - Lahore in 1971 and further one-year specialization in Drilling Operations, Well Planning (Cementation, Drilling Fluid, Bit hydraulics etc.) and HSE from Oklahoma Institute, USA in 1983.

Mr.Jamil has over 45 years of experience in drilling more than 250 onshore &offshore wells from various multinationals E & P entities including Saudi Aramco and OGDCL. He has been vested with several performance records, is responsible for setting new benchmarks in all areas of Drilling Operations and has won numerous performance awards from both multinational & national organizations during his tenure from 1973 to 2006.

Currently, being a part of PEL for more than over a decade, Mr. Jamil successfully completed over dozens of wells in timely and cost-effective manner.

Babar Majid

General Manager Business Development

Babar Majid has over 25 years+ extensive exposure (22 years+ in Pakistan Oil & Gas sector) to the diverse and key dimensions of business management functions. A Board of Directors’ level professional who has served on the Board of Directors of two public sector companies. Besides, having worked at senior level in corporate and government sector, Mr. Majid is skilled in business and regulatory level strategic planning, monitoring and execution, organizational revamp & restructuring, international commercial negotiation, B2B and G2G large scale and capital-intensive infrastructure projects, project financing, operational economics & revenue optimization, energy pricing, forecasting, commercial appraisals, economic & financial modelling, etc.

Mr. Majid holds MBA from University of Central Punjab, Lahore (1996). During career progression, he held various positions in Pakistan Oil & Gas, Real Estate Investment Management and Mineral Sectors. Mr. Majid also remained engaged as a Consultant with an Omani Oil & Gas company and two Chinese conglomerates. He has represented country on multiple international forums such as CPEC, ECO, SAARC, TAPI and IPI projects, Oil Refinery with ADNOC and Saudi Aramco.

While working as General Manager (Business Development, Sales & Marketing) with Pakistan Mineral Development Corporation (PMDC) in 2020, Mr. Majid was an integral member of team which secured the Geographical Indication Protection (GIP) for Pakistan’s Himalayan Pink Rock Salt in international market through World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) under World Trade Organization (WTO). Earlier, he was nominated as Secretary / Member of Pakistan’s first high level Joint LNG Import Working Group (JLNGIWG) constituted by President of Pakistan in 2009 for Mashal LNG Project. In addition, he was also appointed as member of high-level Coordination Committee constituted by Prime Minister of Pakistan in 2019 for development of new oil refining projects in the country with foreign investments. Also acted as resource person to Petroleum Division, GoP on Oil & Gas projects under China Pakistan Economic Corridor- CPEC (2014 -2020).

Mr. Majid has completed / participated in strategic assignments in Pakistan Oil & Gas sector; namely, acquisition of National Refinery Ltd by Attock Group (2004), establishment of Attock Generation Ltd in 2005, transactions structuring, governmental and commercial contractual framework related to IP and TAPI Gas pipelines (2007 - 2014), Tarujabba - Machike Oil Pipeline project (2003 & 2015), Strategic Petroleum Reserves (2015), PARCO Coastal Refinery Project, Saudi Aramco Refinery Project (2016 - 2020), Deferred Oil payment facility of US$3.2 billion arranged on G2G basis with Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for procurement of Saudi origin crude oil and petroleum products by local refineries and PSO

He also coordinated and successfully implemented various BPR and BMR assignments and is experienced in ERP implementation.

Haider Ali Shah

Senior Manager Exploration

Haider Ali Shah graduated from the Center of Excellence in Geology, University of Peshawar and completed Master’s Degree in Geology with specialization in Exploration Geophysics from the University of Southwestern Louisiana, USA under a Fulbright Scholarship.

Mr. Shah has a diversified experience of more than 48 years in the country’s E& P sector. He joined AMOCO Pakistan Exploration Company in 1972 as Geologist and carried out extensive Field Geological mapping projects in the Fold Belts as well as Well Site operations. He joined national Oil and Gas Development Company Limited (OGDCL) in 1981 where he, in various supervisory positions accomplished multidisciplinary assignments including Field Geological mapping, Well Site operations, Prospect Generation, Basin and Petroleum System analysis.

Mr. Shah joined Pakistan Petroleum Limited (PPL) in 2010 as Exploration Consultant where he carried out evaluation of blocks, coordination with the exploration teams and peer reviews. Since 2011 he has been working with Petroleum Exploration (Private) Limited (PEL) as Manager Exploration I/C Badin and Offshore Indus blocks, responsible for their technical evaluation, drilling programs and wellsite operations.
